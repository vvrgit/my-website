About

Dr. Venkataramana Veeramsetty is a Professor in the School of Computer Science and AI. His research focuses on machine learning, intelligent systems, and applications of AI to real-world problems. He has published extensively on topics including (but not limited to) deep learning, explainable AI, and computational algorithms for large-scale data.
